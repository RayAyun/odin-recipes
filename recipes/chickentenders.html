Fried Chicken Tenders

The recipe at a glance

Chicken tenders are a classic dish to get at restaurants. Who doesn't love the delicious taste and juiciness of these culinary delights? Well now you can make it right in your home!

Frying chicken tenders is really simple. You just have to know how to do proper breading on the chicken. You'll need your flour to be seasoned well too. Ready to make some 'tendies?

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablesppons water
  • 2 cups italian-style seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 24 chicken tenderloins
  • 2 quarts oil for frying

Instructions for cooking

  1. prepare the breading station: place flour in a shallow bowl. Place eggs and water in a second shallow bowl. Place bread crumbs in a third shallow bowl, and mix in ground black pepper and cayenne pepper.
  2. Coat chicken in flour, then eggs, and then bread crumbs, one piece at a time, and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees Fahrenheit(190 degress Celsius)
  4. Fry chicken in small batches until the pieces are golden brown, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove chicken to drain on paper towels or a wire rack.
  5. Serve the chicken hot and enjoy!
